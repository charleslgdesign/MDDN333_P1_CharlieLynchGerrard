 This chart is a recording of my screen usage over 20 days.

Emergant patterns can be found in periods where I am busy with audio work. These are followed by days that are spent doing more university. Another pattern that I'm observant of is that time spent on Spotify tends to fluctuate inverted to FL Studio. This makes alot of sense considering that audio work cannot be done at the same time as listening to music, however the opposite tends to happen with school work. I would describe this as a rebounding pattern because of the contrast particularly between Google and FL Studio.

Similarly, sequences like these are present in trading markets because of the tension between the sellers and buyers. For example, the momentum of a stock going up in price becomes more likely to go downwards due to the downward buy pressure.

This dataset has given me a sense of clarity for future budgeting of time. This is particularly important as a student where life can get really busy. To further this project, I would be interested in tracking this data over a longer period of time.
